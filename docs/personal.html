Personal Statement

My sincerest desire to become a Professor of Computer Science growing up with deep and sound knowledge of Mathematics right from my elementary school to high school has really helped me to enjoy and be very delightful in solving complex problems. This has been my dream and driving force behind my chosen field of career which led to my keen interest in studying computer science, ways of logical processing and complex problem solving.

In pursuing computer science I have been fascinated by technologies in general and computers in particular from the beginning. In fact, I quickly picked up interest in programming/software development starting with DOS, QBasic, FORTRAN, COBOL, Pascal, C-language etc as soon as I got admission into high institution.

In furthering my keen desire for computer science, I am having a Master in Social Data Analytics and Research from University of Texas at Dallas in view. I had Master in Business Computing (2018) from University of Ibadan – Nigeria.  To complement my business knowledge and exposure, and develop and manage a robust financial application, I had Master of Business Administration – (2008) from Ladoke Akintola University of Technology – Nigeria. Prior to Master degrees, I graduated with Post Graduate Diploma in Computer Science (2008) from Lagos State University, Nigeria. I graduated from Computer science (2001) major with degree from Yaba College of Technology, Nigeria where I began my Computer Science journey. I am Chartered IT Professional and a Member of Computer Professionals (Registration Council of Nigeria) and a Fellow of Nigeria Computer Society (NCS). I am also a Member of Association for Computing Machinery.

My working career gave me opportunity to develop strategies, oversee the design, development and implementation of major systems initiatives, manage and oversee multiple, concurrent and often disparate tasks for projects involving innovative software applications and highly complex system integration.

I am a dynamic and seasoned IT professional with cognate work experience gained through several developed & managed IT projects. I have my core competence in Software Development, Database Management and Data Science. Over the years working as an IT Professional, IT Manager and IT Consultant, I have exposure and ground knowledge in Database Management, Software development/Programming, System Analysis & Design, System Auditing, Networking, Web Designing/development, Information/Computer Security, and Business Continuity & Disaster Recovery Management. Some of the Software I built and in use are Accounting Management System, Hospital Operation Management System, Sales/Procurement Management System, Facility Booking System, Financial Remittance/Reporting System, School Management System, Homecell Reports Management System, E-Billing/Revenue Management System, Membership Management Portal, Personnel & Payroll Management System.

I gladly excelled and acquired deep knowledge in this my choosing course of studies which I have partly educated my classmates, colleagues in form of tutorials, practicals, capacity building.

Upon achieving my objectives, I wish to further my dreams by continuing studies with Doctor of Philosophy - PhD (Computer Science) with focus on Data Science at University of Texas at Arlington.

Research has been an integral part of my professional development. I specialized in software development, database management and data analytics to mention a few. My history in teaching, research, and impacting IT knowledge blend well with my degrees and open doors to study further in Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) in Computer Science with focus on Data Science.

In the rapidly evolving landscape of technology, I have been captivated by the power of data to drive innovation and transform industries. With a fervent desire to explore the frontiers of knowledge, I have applied for the Computer Science PhD program with a dedicated focus on Data Science. My academic journey and professional experiences have prepared me to embark on this rigorous path of research and discovery.

Whatever mind can conceive, it can achieve with strong determination and focus.
